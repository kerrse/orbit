Location

Orbit needs location permission to confirm your city and show nearby Happenings and clubs. If permission was denied, enable it in your device settings.

QR Friend Adds

QR scanning uses camera permission only when you choose to scan. If scanning fails, confirm camera access and try again in good lighting.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from the profile screen in the app. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, friendships, clubs, Happenings, and avatar uploads where supported.
